How its done

In order to ensure transparency in the 2025/2026 admission exercise the following calculation formula will be used for the exercise:

UTME CANDIDATES:

Candidates will be screened based on their UTME scores and O'level results for the four subjects taken in UTME

The following considerations will apply:

A. RATIO :

The ratio will be 70% UTME score and 30% O'level subject score

B. RANKING OF O'LEVEL RESULTS SCORES :

O'level results will be ranked as given below:

A1=90, B2=80, B3=70, C4=60, C5=55, C6=50, D7=40, E8=30 and F9=0, Awaiting Result (AR)=0

for each candidate, the O'level subjects used will be the subjects the candidate sat for in UTME.

E.g for a candidate who sat for English, Biology, Physics and Chemistry in UTME,

the grades obtained in candidate O'level results in these subjects will be used to calculate the O'level points obtained.

C. BONUS :

A candidate who credited his four O'level subjects in a single sitting will get a bonus of 10 points (i.e Bonus=10),

A candidate who credited his four O'level subjects in two sittings will get no credit (i.e Bonus =0).

D. CALCULATIONS :

Final score = (UTME score*0.7) + (O'level Score + Bonus)*0.3
